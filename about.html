Who We Are

Passionate About Perfect Weddings

Atarashi's Events was founded with a simple yet profound belief: every love story deserves to be celebrated in a way that is as unique and beautiful as the couple themselves. Based in Lapu-Lapu City, Philippines, what started as a small passion project has grown into one of the most sought-after wedding planning companies in Cebu.

Our journey began over 15 years ago when our founder, inspired by the magic of her own wedding, decided to dedicate her life to helping other couples experience that same joy. Today, we are a team of passionate planners, creative designers, and meticulous coordinators who share one common goal—to make your dream wedding a breathtaking reality.

We don't just plan weddings; we craft experiences. From the moment you walk through our doors to the final dance at your reception, we are by your side, ensuring every detail is perfect and every moment is magical.
