FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should we book your services?

We recommend booking 12-18 months in advance for full wedding planning services. However, we can accommodate shorter timelines depending on our availability. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

What is included in the initial consultation?

Our complimentary initial consultation includes a detailed discussion of your vision, budget considerations, venue options, and an overview of our services. It's an opportunity for us to get to know each other.

Do you work with specific vendors only?

We have a curated network of trusted vendors, but we're happy to work with vendors of your choice. Our goal is to create your dream wedding, and we'll collaborate with any professionals who can help achieve that vision.

Can you plan destination weddings?

Absolutely! We specialize in both local and destination weddings. Our team has experience planning celebrations in various locations throughout the Philippines and can handle all the logistics.
